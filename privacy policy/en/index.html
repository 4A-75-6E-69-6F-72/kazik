Privacy Policy for Kazik Music Player

Updated on: 11/11/2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to the app Kazik. This privacy policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use and protect your information when you use the application.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Kazik is an offline music app and does not collect any user information, including personal or device-related information. We do not track your usage, preferences or any other data.

3. Data storage

All music content and data used by the app is stored locally on your device. We do not store any data on our servers or in cloud storage.

4. Offline use

Kazik is designed to work completely offline. It does not require an Internet connection for its basic functionality. If you are connected to the internet, advertisements from Google Ads are displayed. These follow their policy available here

5. Advertising and Data Collection for Google Ads

The Kazik app uses Google Ads to display relevant advertisements to users. Google Ads may collect certain information when you interact with advertisements displayed in the application. This information may include data such as ad impressions, clicks about announcements, and information about your device.

Please note that data collection by Google Ads is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. To learn more about how Google Ads uses data, you can read the Google Ads privacy policy available at https://policies.google.com/privacy .

It is important to note that Kazik does not have access to personal information collected by Google Ads. We do not share specific user data with third parties for advertising purposes. By using the Kazik app, you consent to the collection of data by Google Ads in accordance with the Google Ads privacy policy.

6. Children's privacy

Kazik does not knowingly collect personal information from persons under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can delete this information.

7. Changes to this privacy policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any modification will be effective upon posting of the revised Privacy Policy, and your continued use of the application after these changes indicates your consent to the updated terms.

8. Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at ngouoghe.junior@ictuniversity.edu.cm.
